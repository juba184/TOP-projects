Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious recipe for a classic italian lasagne with beef. Perfect for a family dinner or a cozy evening with friends.

Ingredients

  • carrots
  • onions
  • beef
  • Celery
  • Tomato paste
  • chopped tomatoes
  • wine
  • lasagna plates
  • cheese
  • milk
  • flour
  • butter

Steps

  1. chop and fry the carrots, Celery and onions first
  2. add garlic tomato paste to the ingredients
  3. add the beef
  4. deglaze with wine
  5. add choped tomatoes and spices
  6. let the butter melt in an seperate pot
  7. slowly add the flour to it
  8. when it thickend slowly pour the milk sip by sip into it
  9. spice it up with salt, pepper and Nutmeg
  10. put it all together layer by layer in a large mold. Starting with the sauce, bechamel sauce, then the lasagne plates
  11. put it in the oven for 35 minutes at 180Â°C Circulation
  12. put cheese ontop of it and put it in the oven again for 10 minutes

Egg sandwich

Description

This is a delicious recipe for a as simple as delicious sandwich Perfect if you need something to eat but don't have much time.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • spring onions
  • cheese
  • Srirache Mayo
  • Toast

Steps

  1. fry the eggs in a pan
  2. put them aside and fry the spring onions
  3. put them asside to and add the toast in the pan aswell

  4. when the bread is toasted, add cheese, then the two eggs, the spring onions, then again cheese, sriracha mayo and ontop the second slice of bread
